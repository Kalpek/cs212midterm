The Bridge

This is the beginning of the bridge...

This project by far has taken me the longest to complete. I began immediately after finishing Lab 6, and its been a good few hours of trial and error. The biggest challenge was definitely the different paths between in game.

Creating and keeping track of the path without something freezing was more difficult than I thought it'd be for sure. I always felt like there was something I forgot, then when I felt like I had everything, something froze the page and I couldn't figure out why.

I chose a bridge game because of some mario party nostalgia, but it also just wasn't anything too crazy that still lead to a different outcome. Honestly the worst part about these back to back projects is that they were due on the same day, and I just so happen to be very sick.

But overall, it was enjoyable to finally get the end product I wanted.
